Deploying on Netlify is simple

Follow the steps below to get your WhataDuck instance up and running on Netlify or just click the deploy button.
<NetlifyDeploy
  repositoryUrl="https://github.com/MandavkarPranjal/whataduck"
  projectName="whataduck"
/>

## Steps
<Steps>
    <Step>
        Fork the repository:
        [Create a fork](https://github.com/MandavkarPranjal/whataduck/fork)
    </Step>
    <Step>
        Create a new Project on Netlify:
        https://app.netlify.com/start
    </Step>
    <Step>
        Connect your GitHub account
    </Step>
    <Step>
        Select WhataDuck repo & click on Deploy
    </Step>
</Steps>
that's it!
