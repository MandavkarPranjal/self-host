Deploying on Vercel is quick and easy with *zero configuration* required.

Follow the steps below to get your WhataDuck instance up and running on Vercel or just click the deploy button.
<VercelDeploy
  repositoryUrl="https://github.com/MandavkarPranjal/whataduck"
  projectName="whataduck"
/>

## Steps
<Steps>
    <Step>
        #### Fork the repository
        [Create a fork](https://github.com/MandavkarPranjal/whataduck/fork)
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Create a new Project on Vercel
        https://vercel.com/new/project
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Select WhataDuck repo & click on Deploy
        that's it!
    </Step>
</Steps>
